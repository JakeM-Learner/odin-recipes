Jake's Braised Brisket

Description

A succulent, juicy, melt-in-your-mouth brisket braised with root vegetables and celery.

Ingredients

  • 6 lbs Brisket (point preferred)
  • 3 medium Yellow Onions (quartered)
  • 3 medium Yellow Onions (sliced)
  • 6 large Carrots (large dice)
  • 1/2 head Celery (large dice)
  • 1 lb small Youkon Gold Potatos
  • 2 tbsp Garlic (chopped)
  • 1 cup Red Wine
  • 1 large can (16oz) Whole Peeled Tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Ketchup
  • 4 sprigs Fresh Thyme
  • 3 large Bay Leaves
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • Salt to Taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F
  2. Brown brisket on all sides in Butter over medium-high heat
  3. Layer sliced Onions on bottom of large roasting pan
  4. Deglaze pan with Red Wine
  5. Place brisket on bed of Onions
  6. Combine all other ingredients and spread around brisket in roasting pan
  7. Braise in oven @ 300 degrees F for 4-6 hours until probe tender
  8. Let rest 30min, slice, and place back in braising juices to serve
