Granola

Description

A sweet and slightly salty granola perfect for topping yogurt or enjoying with milk.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Rolled Oats
  • 2 cups Chopped Nuts (optional)
  • 1 large dash Salt
  • 2 tbsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • 1/2 cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups Crasins

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Combine all ingredients except Crasins in a large mixing bowl
  3. Spread mixture on 1/2 size aluminum baking sheet and bake for 22 min, stirring 1/2 way
  4. Remove from oven and let cool. Mix in 2 cups Crasins.
