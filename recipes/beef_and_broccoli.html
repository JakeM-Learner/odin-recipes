Jake's Beef and Broccoli

Description

A mouthwatering dish composed of thinnly sliced beef sauted with broccoli in a sweet and savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Beef Sirloin
  • 1 Large Head Broccoli - Chopped into Florets
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Ginger - Shredded
  • 1 tbsp Garlic - Chopped
  • 6 tbsp Soy Sauce or Tamari
  • 2 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 3 tbsp Light Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Corn Starch
  • Black Pepper to Taste
  • 1/2 Cup Warm Water
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil

Instructions

  1. Brown beef in Cooking Oil on high heat
  2. Add Broccoli, cook until dark green
  3. Whisk together all other ingredients
  4. Add to pan and simmer approx 5 min on low heat
  5. Serve over rice
